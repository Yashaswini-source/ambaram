The Heart of Our Celebrations

AMBARAM is more than just an organization; it's the beating heart of the Indian community in Tampa. We specialize in creating immersive festive environments where entertainment meets tradition. Our goal is to host events that aren't just attended, but truly lived.


Whether it's the colors of Holi, the lights of Diwali, or the traditional harvest joy of Sankranthi, we curate every detail. From organized competitions with exciting prizes to stage performances that leave you mesmerized, we ensure every guest feels the warmth of our heritage.
